  1. Increased Motivation: Racing against a previous best performance creates an innate competitive spirit within you. Inspired to run faster, or maybe even try to beat that ghost? Bringing that competitive edge into your workouts can be just the spark you need.

  3. Personal Challenges: Ghost Runs allow you to set personalized challenges based on your own benchmarks. Beat your last time? Consider it a win! This self-pacing sticks with you, motivating you to improve with every run.

  4. Setting the Stage for Focus: Exercise is known to boost focus, and with Ghost Runs, you can harness that focus during workouts and carry it over into your work. A good run clears your head, allowing you to dive into tasks when you get back home or into the office.
