Understanding Zero-Lag Training

Zero-Lag Training is rooted in the philosophy that every minor delay can lead to decreased motivation and suboptimal performance. In a world where most fitness trackers require an internet connection, syncing your stats can feel tedious and disruptive, drawing your focus away from your workout.

Imagine this scenario: you've just completed a grueling workout, and you’re ready to log your data. Instead of pulling out your phone, connecting to Wi-Fi, and waiting for your app to update, you can jot everything down immediately in your gym log. This is where local gym logs shine—allowing for instant feedback on your performance without the lag.
