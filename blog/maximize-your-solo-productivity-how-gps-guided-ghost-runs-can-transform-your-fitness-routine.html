What Are Ghost Runs, Anyway?

You might be wondering what a ghost run even is. Picture this: you lace up your favorite pair of running shoes, step outside into the crisp morning air, and race your best self from the past. Yep! A ghost run uses GPS technology to track your performance against your previous records, pushing you not just to keep pace, but to outshine your former self. This isn't just a run; it’s a competition between you and—you guessed it—yourself!

Whether you’re looking to beat your last 5K time or finally conquer that dreaded hill, ghost runs offer an exciting twist that makes fitness less about competition with others and more about self-improvement.
