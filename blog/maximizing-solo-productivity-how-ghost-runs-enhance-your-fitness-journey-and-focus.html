What are Ghost Runs?

Now, let’s get to why you clicked on this blog post—Ghost Runs! Imagine running through your favorite path only to find you’re in a race… against yourself. Cool, right? Ghost Runs track your previous best times and create a virtual competitor to beat. This means every time you hit the pavement, you’re not just running; you’re challenging your previous self to go harder and faster.

How Ghost Runs Work

Using Ghost Runs is as straightforward as it gets. Once you've logged a workout with your fastest time, the app stores that information and uses it to create a “ghost” of yourself. Whenever you decide to step out for a run, this ghost runs alongside you, showing your previous pace, distance, and the finish time you need to beat. Talk about motivation on steroids!

  1. Setting It Up: Simply use the Fitness Suite to log a run or workout. Make sure to enjoy that early morning or late evening fresh air—trust me, it's therapeutic!

  2. Track Progress: Each time you run, your ghost will pop up on the route. You’ll notice how you stack up against your last performance. Did it rain last time? Did you crush that heel section? Whatever it is, you'll see in real-time how you measure up.

  3. Challenge Yourself: By pitting yourself against your past self, you're constantly raising the stakes. It can be downright exhilarating to push through and beat that previous best time.
