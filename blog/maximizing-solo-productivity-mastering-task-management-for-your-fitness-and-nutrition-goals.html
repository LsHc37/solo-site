If there’s one feature that’s going to become your best buddy, it's the To-Do Rack. Think of it as a focused checklist for your daily success. It’s simple but oh-so-effective. Here’s how to make the most out of it:

1. Prioritize Tasks

When setting your tasks for the day, it’s important to prioritize. Are you focusing on your fitness or prepping nutritious meals today? Using the To-Do Rack, select those priorities and compartmentalize them.

For example:

  • Fitness Tasks:

  • Weight Training: Upper Body

  • Yoga: 20 mins

  • Nutrition Tasks:

  • Grocery shopping for healthy snacks

2. Use Reminders

Set reminders to nudge you. Let’s be honest: life is busy, and sometimes you need a gentle push. Solo Productivity lets you set these reminders offline, which means you can carry your motivation anywhere without distractions buzzing in your pocket.

3. Daily Check-Ins

At the end of the day, check off what you accomplished and reflect on what went well and what didn’t. This is your time to learn, adapt, and improve for tomorrow.
