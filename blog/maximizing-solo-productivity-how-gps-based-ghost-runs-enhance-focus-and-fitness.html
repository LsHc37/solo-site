The Magic of Ghost Runs

Now, let’s get to the party trick: ghost runs. If you’re wondering what a ghost run is, let me explain. It’s essentially a way of racing against your past self. How cool is that?

Get Competitive with Yourself

Ghost runs give you a unique opportunity to challenge yourself. When you run a route you’ve previously completed, Solo Productivity's GPS tracking allows you to compare your current time against your past best. It’s like having a virtual training partner who is consistently motivating you to push your limits.

How Ghost Runs Work: 1. Select a Route: Choose a running trail or path you’ve previously completed. 2. Start the Ghost Run: Solo Productivity will use GPS to track your current run. 3. Race Against Your Past: As you run, the app reveals your previous time, acting as your ghostly opponent.

This little feature turns a simple jog into a genuine challenge—each step is measured against your own performance, making it a fun yet effective way to stay motivated.
